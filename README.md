# Kyungsung Energy Workshop

Teaching materials and reduced sample datasets for an undergraduate energy problem-solving workshop.

## Contents

- `data/electricity-demand-sample.csv`: electricity supply and demand teaching sample.
- `data/solar-weather-sample.csv`: Jeju solar generation and weather teaching sample.
- `data/energy-use-sample.csv`: sector-level energy use teaching sample.
- `outputs/energy-problem-solution-workshop.pptx`: workshop slide deck in the local project folder.

## Data Notes

The CSV files are small classroom examples. They preserve public-source column concepts and units, but they are reduced teaching samples rather than official historical datasets.

## Public-Source Framing

- Korea Power Exchange: https://www.kpx.or.kr/eng/
- Korea public data portal: https://www.data.go.kr/en/data/15143884/fileData.do
- Korea Energy Agency: https://www.energy.or.kr/en/main/main.do
- Korea Energy Statistical Information System: https://kesis.keei.re.kr/eng

## Privacy

The materials do not include student records, personal information, customer-level meter data, addresses, facility identifiers, or administrative university data.
